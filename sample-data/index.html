Key Features

6-File Upload System

Upload Transaction Summary, BCA Statement, and 4 master files for comprehensive reconciliation.

5 Transaction Types

Supports CDM, Credit Card, QRIS, Debit Card, and Setoran Tunai matching logic.

Commission Calculation

Automatic merchant rate lookup and commission deduction with priority matching.

Date-1 Logic

Matches BCA transactions with ACMM records from the previous day automatically.

Smart Pivoting

Groups transactions by date, outlet, and payment method for accurate matching.

Multiple Exports

Export matched, unmatched, or specific transaction types to Excel.

How to Use

1

Download Sample Files

Click "Download Sample Files" button above to access the sample data generator. Download all 6 Excel files.

2

Launch the Tool

Click "Launch Sales Reconciliation Tool" to open the reconciliation interface.

3

Upload Files

Upload the downloaded sample files in their respective slots. Minimum requirement: BCA Statement.

4

Start Reconciliation

Click "Start Reconciliation Process" and watch the magic happen!

5

Review Results

View matched/unmatched records, filter by transaction type, and export results.

  • Smart Matching: Uses Map-based lookups for fast performance
  • Flexible Exports: Download results in Excel format
  • Responsive Design: Works on desktop, tablet, and mobile
